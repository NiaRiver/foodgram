Welcome to AlertTrade

AlertTrade automates trading by processing TradingView alerts and executing orders on various exchanges.

The platform accepts API requests, processes text data, and based on the contents of the alerts, sends the necessary requests to place trading orders on the selected exchange.

This significantly simplifies and speeds up the trading process, making it more efficient and automated
